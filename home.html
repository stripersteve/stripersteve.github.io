     Welcome to Fishing Lake Lanier with Striper Steve’s Fishing     
     Charters and Guide Services, based in beautiful North Georgia     
     on Lake Lanier. Lake Lanier Fishing Guides available year around.     
     Lake Lanier is known for its world class freshwater fishing.     
     As my name says, I focus on the mighty Striped Bass, or as we     
     call ’em around here, “Stripers”. Lake Lanier is located an hour     
     north of Atlanta and offers 40,000 acres of exciting action for     
     Stripers ranging from a few pounds up to the lake record 48     
     pounder. We average fish in the 8-25 lbs range and have many days     
     with double digit bags.
